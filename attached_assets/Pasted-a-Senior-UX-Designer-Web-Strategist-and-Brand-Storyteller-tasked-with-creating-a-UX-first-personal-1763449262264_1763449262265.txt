a Senior UX Designer, Web Strategist, and Brand Storyteller tasked with creating a UX-first personal portfolio website for Angela Rose Boyle (ARB) — a professional UX/UI Specialist at a Fortune 500 company. The website must demonstrate strong UX thinking, clean visual hierarchy, and emotional storytelling that immediately impresses hiring managers while laying the foundation for future freelance work.

Objective:
Design and structure a website that:

Showcases Angela’s UX expertise, process, and credibility to recruiters and design leads.

Introduces her freelance brand Divine Align Design for future consulting leads.

Embodies her aesthetic — modern × whimsical × fantasy-meets-professional — while maintaining polish and clarity.

Core Brand Attributes:

Tone: Cool, confident, human

Visual Feel: Teal + gold palette, orchid-pink accent, celestial/vine motifs

Mood: Whimsical + elevated + refined minimalism

Tagline Concept: Where logic meets imagination

Target Audience:
Primary – UX design hiring managers and recruiters
Secondary – Freelance UX/consulting clients and creative collaborators

UX Principles to Prioritize:

Clear information architecture with intuitive navigation

Consistent hierarchy and spacing

Accessibility (contrast, alt text, readable typography)

Mobile-first responsive design

Emotional design: warmth + credibility balance

Storytelling through process and visual flow

Website Architecture (6 Pages + Supporting Elements):

Home – Hero statement, brief bio, primary CTA (“View Case Studies”), secondary CTA (“Work With Me”). Animated hero or subtle scroll effect.

Case Studies (4 Total) – Individual project pages with consistent UX case-study structure:

Overview (problem, role, timeline)

Process (research → ideation → design → testing)

Impact (results, metrics, learnings)

Visual mockups for mobile & desktop

Optional interactive prototype embed

About Me – Personal story, global experience, transition from pro soccer to UX; focus on empathy, leadership, adaptability. Include professional headshot.

Services / Consulting (Freelance Preview) – Intro to Divine Align Design: UX Audits, Interface Redesigns, Product Strategy, Brand Consulting. Clear “Contact for Consulting” CTA.

Gallery / Creative Work – Select modeling & fantasy-themed photoshoots showcasing personal creativity and brand aesthetic without distracting from UX professionalism.

Contact – Clean, easy form (Name / Email / Reason for Contact dropdown); include LinkedIn + email + optional Calendly integration.

Navigation & IA Recommendations:

Persistent top nav: Home | Case Studies | About | Services | Gallery | Contact

Sticky CTA in nav: “View My Work”

Footer: condensed nav + copyright + social icons

Visual & UI Guidelines:

Typography: modern serif + clean sans pairing (e.g., Playfair Display + Inter)

Motion: subtle parallax or fade transitions to convey sophistication

Imagery: blend professional UX visuals (screens, wireframes) with soft fantasy backgrounds (stars, light texture)

Components: card layouts, iconography for UX deliverables, and grid alignment

Deliverables Expected from You (the AI):

Full sitemap + IA diagram

Page-by-page content outline with UX rationale

Suggested copy tone and microcopy examples

Visual style system (colors, typography, spacing, motion guidelines)

Optional Figma-ready component guide or Squarespace implementation plan

Accessibility checklist + SEO optimization outline (keywords: UX Designer Angela Boyle, UX Portfolio, UX Consultant, Divine Align Design)

Constraints:

Must be professional first, creative second

Clear hierarchy > ornamentation

Content brevity and scannability are critical

Stretch Goals (optional):

Add subtle “Experience Map” or “Timeline” animation on About page

Incorporate AI chat or contact assistant (“Ask Angela”) for engagement

Integrate blog or “Insights” section later for thought leadership

Deliver the output as:

Structured design brief with visual + content recommendations

Highlight how each section satisfies UX design principles and hiring-manager expectations
